Definition:

Website hosting is a service that allows individuals or organizations to publish their website on the internet.
Hosting providers store the website’s files, databases, and content on their servers.

Features of good hosting:
Reliability/Uptime – website should always be accessible
Storage space – enough for files and databases
Bandwidth – amount of data transferred to visitors
Security – protection from hacking and malware
Support – technical help from hosting provider

Importance of website hosting:
Makes your website accessible globally
Ensures fast loading and performance
Provides backup and security options
Helps with scalability as your site grows

Examples of hosting providers:
Bluehost
HostGator
SiteGround
GoDaddy
AWS (Amazon Web Services)
